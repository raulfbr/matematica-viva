## 1. Purpose

Validate that every lesson respects the child as a person and follows CM principles.

---

## 2. The 20 Principles (SSOT: MAGNA_CARTA lines 107-133)

| # | Principle | Application |
|:--|:----------|:------------|
| 1 | *"Children are born persons."* | Treat child as complete person |
| 2 | *"Not born good or bad..."* | Education forms, doesn't just inform |
| 3 | *"Authority and obedience..."* | Parents have legitimate authority |
| 4 | *"Authority is not license."* | Authority respects the person |
| 5 | *"Atmosphere, discipline, life."* | The 3 instruments in each lesson |
| 6 | *"Atmosphere is natural..."* | Don't create artificial atmosphere |
| 7 | *"Discipline of habits..."* | Form mental/moral habits |
| 8 | *"Life = living ideas..."* | **LIVING IDEAS required** |
| 9 | *"Mind feeds on ideas..."* | Mind digests ideas |
| 10 | *"Generous curriculum."* | Broad and rich curriculum |
| 11 | *"Knowledge through curriculum."* | Curriculum is vehicle |
| 12 | *"Science of relations."* | Connect math with life |
| 13 | *"Short lessons."* | **â‰¤20 min (Seeds: 15-20)** |
| 14 | *"Narration..."* | **Child retells** |

| 17 | *"No rivalry."* | Cooperation > Competition |

## 3. The 5 Audit Questions

| # | Question | Source |
|---|----------|--------|
| 1 | Is the child respected as a capable person? | CM Principle 1 |
| 2 | Is the Habit of Attention preserved (short lesson)? | CM Principle 13 |
| 3 | Things before Signs: Is CPA used (Concrete first)? | Singapore/Bruner |
| 4 | Is there space for Narration at the end? | CM Principle 14 |
| 5 | Is the Living Idea "presented" (not "explained")? | CM Principle 8 |

---

## 4. Audit Procedure

### Step 1: Read the lesson once
- [ ] Read completely without taking notes

### Step 2: Answer the 5 questions
- [ ] Fill in checklist honestly

### Step 3: Identify issues
- [ ] List specific problems found

### Step 4: Generate report
- [ ] Use the QA report template
- [ ] Recommend APPROVED/REJECTED

---

## 5. Red Flags (Automatic Rejection)

- Lesson > 25 minutes
- No Living Idea identifiable
- CPA out of order
- Condescending tone
- No narration space
